What is Reachout?

It is a Chrome extension+Depression Analyser for social media sites, to help users struggling with their mental health.

Chrome Extension

Serves as a preventative tool to detect harmful posts on social media before they go live. These posts include content with trigger warnings, descriptions of self-harm and suicidal ideation, and anything that suggests a user is struggling with their mental health.

Web App

Serves as a tool that would enable a user to analyse his/her social media for depressive content.
